News

Annual Mountain Music Festival

June 10, 2024 - Eagleton's beloved Mountain Music Festival returns this summer at Mountain Village. Enjoy live performances from local bands, artisan crafts, and delicious food. Tickets are available now!

New Tech Hub Opens Downtown

May 25, 2024 - Eagleton welcomes its latest addition to the business community with the grand opening of the Eagleton Innovation Center. This state-of-the-art facility aims to attract tech startups and foster innovation.

Eagleton Peaks Record

May 15, 2024 - Eagleton Peaks National Park saw a record number of visitors this spring, with over 100,000 nature enthusiasts exploring the trails and wildlife. Park officials plan to expand facilities to accommodate the growing numbers.

Eagle Ridge Ski Resort Plans Expansion

May 3, 2024 - Eagle Ridge Ski Resort announces plans to expand its facilities with new slopes and a larger lodge. Construction will begin this fall, with new amenities available next ski season.
