Applicant Information
Membership Level
Select your membership:

Gold Membership

$600 Annually

  • All Silver level benefits
  • Exclusive Event Invitations: Invitations to VIP networking events and exclusive receptions with community leaders and business influencers.
  • Advertising Benefits: Featured spotlight position on the chamber's homepage for one month and premium advertising space in the chamber's monthly newsletter.
  • Priority Access: Priority registration for all events, ensuring members get the first choice on limited-seating events and programs.
  • Annual Business Expo: Free booth at the annual business expo, providing direct exposure to potential clients and partners.
  • Personalized Business Consultation: Two sessions per year with a business advisor for personalized strategic planning and growth advice.
  • Sponsorship Opportunities: Eligibility to sponsor major events, enhancing brand visibility and community presence.
  • Enhanced Online Presence: Premium listing in the online member directory, including a company logo, business description, and direct contact links.

Silver Membership

$300 Annually

  • All Bronze level benefits
  • Training Workshops: Free attendance to four training workshops per year, covering essential business skills and industry-specific knowledge.
  • Webinars: Access to an expanded library of member-only webinars on topics ranging from marketing strategies to financial planning.
  • Enhanced Directory Listing: Enhanced listing in the online member directory with added business information and promotional materials.
  • Event Discounts: Greater discounts on chamber events, including luncheons, seminars, and community gatherings.
  • Newsletter Feature: Opportunity to be featured in the chamber's monthly newsletter, reaching a wide audience of local businesses and stakeholders.

Bronze Membership

$150 Annually

  • Networking Events: Access to monthly networking events, providing opportunities to connect with other local businesses and community leaders.
  • Basic Directory Listing: Basic listing in the online member directory with business name and contact information.
  • Event Discounts: Discounts on chamber events, including educational seminars and social gatherings.
  • Resource Access: Access to the chamber's resource library, including industry reports, business templates, and regulatory updates.
  • Promotional Opportunities: Opportunities to promote your business through chamber social media channels and at community events.

Nonprofit Membership

There is no fee.

  • Networking Events: Access to monthly networking events tailored to nonprofits, providing opportunities to connect with potential donors and collaborators.
  • Directory Listing: Basic listing in the online member directory, highlighting nonprofit status and mission.
  • Event Discounts: Discounts on chamber events, particularly those focused on nonprofit management and fundraising.
  • Training Workshops: Access to two free training workshops per year, focusing on nonprofit management, grant writing, and community engagement.
  • Resource Access: Access to the chamber's nonprofit resource library, including funding opportunities, volunteer management tools, and best practices guides.
  • Volunteer Matching: Inclusion in the chamber's volunteer matching program, connecting nonprofits with local volunteers and service organizations.
