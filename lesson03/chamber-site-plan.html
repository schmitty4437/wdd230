Eagle Mountain Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Site name is Eagle Mountain Chamber of Commerce. I chose that name since that is the area I live in and it makes it clear what the site is related to.

Site Purpose

The Eagle Mountain Chamber of Commerce website serves to foster community engagement and promote economic growth. Through our Home page, visitors are greeted with captivating content, community updates, and business spotlights, encouraging exploration. Our Directory connects residents and businesses by listing local chamber-affiliated organizations, while our Join page offers membership benefits tailored to diverse needs, from non-profits to premium perks for businesses. By fulfilling these purposes, our website aims to enhance both the community spirit and economic vitality of Eagle Mountain.

Scenarios

  • Can you provide more details about the different membership levels and benefits for joining the chamber of commerce?
  • Where can visitors get information about local attractions and events happening int eh city?
  • Where can I get more information about local businesses in the area?
