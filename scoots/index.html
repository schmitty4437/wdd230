Who We Are

Welcome to Scoots, your premier motor scooter rental company on Cozumel island in the Caribbean Sea. We cater to locals, tourists, and cruise-based groups with two convenient locations near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock and Terminal Puerta Maya. Our friendly services include motor scooters, jeeps, and ATVs, perfect for exploring both the bustling town and the serene southern and eastern shores. Our website provides all the necessary information to plan your rental in advance, reflecting the island's fun and carefree spirit. Explore Cozumel with ease and excitement with Scoots!

Our Rentals

Scooters

Experience the freedom of Cozumel with our easy-to-ride motor scooters.
